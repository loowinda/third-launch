FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What types of photography do you do?

- We do alot of type of photography such as wedding, maternity, portrait, birthdays, baby shower, prenup and more. Come hit us up, we might have the service you're trying to find. Proceed to our how to reach us page.

Why should I hire a professional photographer?

- We have the expertise and experience. By hiring a professional photographer you get quality images, and it enables you to actually feature your products, services, employees, and happy customers in the photos. A talented photographer know how to produce the photo shoot and create images that show your personality. Taking the responsibility of producing photography off your plate lets you actually be productive and enjoy the moment.

How long will our session take?

- In our case, no. Deposits are non-refundable. We try to ensure that our time and effort to prepare things are worth it. We encourage our clients to think everything through before making decisions that might cause inconvience not only for themselves but also for us.

When will we get our finished prints?

- Usually it's 3-5 business days but it still depends in every type of photography we do and how booked our company is. Rest assured that we try to get done our finished products as soon as we can.

What’s your cancellation/rescheduling policy?

- Cancellations and reschedules are no charge provided they are done 24 hours in advance of the scheduled shoot date/time. If I show up to a property and can’t get in or the shoot is cancelled by the agent/seller, the fee is P500 + 50% of the quoted amount. If I quoted P2,000, the fee would be P1,500. Yes, it’s near the cost of the full shoot, however I wasted my time showing up to the property and to me it’s unacceptable to cancel with no notice so the “punishment” is more firm. Same goes if you cancelled on the day of the shoot and I already prepared our studio for you.

Can we see photography case studies and example portfolio?

How do we mitigate technical issues at the event?

- Photographers have at least two professional camera bodies, several lenses and a multitude of accessories specific to your shoot. Well maintained equipment is very reliable these days so it is very unlikely there will be an impact to capturing great photos at your event.

Who is my contact before and during the event?

- PITIKGRAPHY-Z likes to personally call every client before the event or shoot for a quick chat. We believe personal service works better in person. At the event, the head photographer will be your primary contact point and direct any second shooters. We’ll advise who the head photographer is for your event during the pre-event phone call and emails.
